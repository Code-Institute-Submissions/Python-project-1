About Timely-Care

Timely-Care was started by proffesionals looking to help people who may not be able to go to their local hairdressers or beauty salon either due to medical conditions or age. Our aim is to provide a friendly, easy service to people in need and help support our communites.

We also love taking care of wedding parties where we can provide numerous packages for the bride and her bridesmaid including hairstyles, makeup and nails. It's flexible as you choose the location and we will come to you!

If you are a proffesional and are thinking about joining our lovely team please get in touch! Simply send us your information thorugh our contact page and let us know what you do, we'll be in touch as soon as possible.
